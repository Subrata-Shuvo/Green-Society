Welcome to Green Society

The Green World Campaign has been cited by the Natural Resources Defense Council and others for its bold agenda: Regenerate degraded landscapes around the world. Help struggling communities to thrive using nature-based solutions. Combat climate change in ways that improve the health of our shared biosphere and the harmony of our global village. Our work centers around agro-ecology (combining agriculture with trees and other useful plants). Our flagship office in Kenya has planted 3.75 million trees, established 100 eco-schools programs, and provided farmers’ training, women’s and children’s health, and rural livelihoods.
